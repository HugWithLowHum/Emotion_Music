Zian's Music Generator

Background

This program is inspired by video game No Man's Sky's procedurally generated music.

No Man's Sky is a space exploring game that has 18 quintillion procedurally generated planets, each has its unique minerals, flora, terrain, etc. The developers wanted to create a game that leaves players infinite worlds to explore. This idea was also reflected by the game's background music, created by the music group 65daysofstatic. You will never hear the exact same piece of music twice in the game. Instead, the music you hear will always be modifications of several motifs. The music changes according to the player's environment, behavior, and status. For instance, the music on a freezing planet with monsters that hunt the player might share similar melodic idea with the music on a peaceful earth-like planet, but the former will sound more intense and unsettling.

I wanted to take this idea of "music generation based on user experience" and create a personalized music generation program. This program generates music that reflects the user's emotion. User may input their emotional state by selecting a point on the Circumplex of Emotion by Russel (1980).
